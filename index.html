About Us

Vedic Techno School is a premier institution providing education from Pre-Nursery to Class 10 under the CBSE pattern. With a focus on holistic learning, we combine modern technology with traditional values, ensuring the all-round development of every student.

Our Facilities

Smart Classrooms

Digital learning with interactive smart boards and audio-visual support.

Hostel with AC Rooms

Comfortable and modern hostel facilities with air-conditioned rooms.

Sports & Activities

Yoga, Taekwondo, Music, Dance, Art & Craft for overall student growth.

Practical Lab Based Learning

Hands-on experiments in Science, Computer, and Mathematics labs to strengthen practical knowledge and innovation starting from Nursery to Class 10.
